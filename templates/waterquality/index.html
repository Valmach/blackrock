Welcome to the Water Quality Module!

Overview

The Graphing Tool allows you to graph various parameters such as rainfall, water temperature, pH, and dissolved oxygen. The tool uses real 2009 data from Black Rock Forest, the Hudson River and the Harlem River. With these graphs, you can compare and deduce how these parameters affect aquatic ecosystems in a rural versus urban setting.
